Our Story

Hi, my name is Caleb Warner. I'm the creator of Brownie Clicker.

I wanted to practice my HTML, CSS, and JavaScript skills. I thought it would be fun to create something like Cookie Clicker to practice JavaScript. On top of that, I wanted to make a professional landing page to practice HTML and CSS. Thus, this website was born.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide the player with a fun experience of clicking a brownie.

And also to practice coding websites.
